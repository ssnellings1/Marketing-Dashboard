Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Marketing Dashboard application ("Application") is an internal business tool used solely by authorized personnel of the operating law firm ("Firm"). This Privacy Policy describes how data is collected, used, and protected within the Application.

1. Data Collected

The Application collects and stores the following data:

  • Marketing spend data imported from QuickBooks Online, Google Ads, and Meta Ads
  • Case intake data imported from case management and CRM systems
  • User login credentials (username and hashed password) for authorized staff
2. Use of Data

All data collected is used exclusively for internal business analytics — specifically to calculate and monitor marketing case acquisition costs. No data is shared with, sold to, or processed by any third party for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Third-Party Integrations

The Application connects to QuickBooks Online via OAuth 2.0 to read financial expense data. Only expense records are accessed — no payment processing or write operations are performed. Access tokens are stored securely and are never shared.

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored in a secured, encrypted database hosted on Railway (railway.app). Access to the Application is protected by username and password authentication. HTTPS is enforced for all connections.

5. Data Retention

Data is retained for as long as it is needed for business analytics purposes. Authorized administrators may delete records at any time through the Application interface.

6. Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the Firm's administrator directly.
